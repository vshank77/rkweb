Sri Radha Madhav Kalyana Mahotsav is an ancient and well-known Indian tradition symbolising the key Vedanta philosophy of each of us being a part of the supreme consciousness. It is also an excellent example of National Integration as several regional cultures and languages are fused into one unique divine event.
