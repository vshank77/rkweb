Sri Radha Madhav Kalyana Mahotsav is an ancient and well-known Indian tradition symbolising the key Vedanta philosophy of each of us being a part of the supreme consciousness. It is also an excellent example of National Integration as several regional cultures and languages are fused into one unique divine event.

Why sponsor this event?

  • Radha Madhava Kalyana Mahotsav on this grand scale is the first of its kind to be held in the UK
  • It is free community event and visitors from all over UK are expected to join the celebrations
  • A large cross section of the Indian diaspora is expected to participate
  • A renowned performer from India has been invited to conduct the main event
  • This is a family event that will appeal to all age groups
  • There will be several small events in and outside London in the preceding week as a build-up to the main event
  • Several dignitaries and media coverage is expected to grace the occasion
  • Website and social media promotion will feature key sponsors
  • Bring this cultural tradition in all its grandeur and large scale for the first time in UK
  • Provide an opportunity to the younger generation understand the significance of Vedanta philosophy
  • Let the community benefit from the joy of Indian tradition, music and vibrancy of the event
