Event Details

Sri Radha Madhav Kalyana Mahotsav is an ancient and well-known Indian tradition symbolising the key Vedanta philosophy of each of us being a part of the supreme consciousness. It is also an excellent example of National Integration as several regional cultures and languages are fused into one unique divine event. We are delighted to be bringing a Radha Kalyana of this scale to the UK for the first time.

This free community event, will create an atmosphere full of celebration and joy as one would experience during a marriage . Vibrant traditional performances & South Indian delicacies will form the backdrop for the the symbolic wedding between Radha and Krishna which will be led by one of the most reputed and well-known Bhagavathar(Traditional/Religious Musician) from India.

Key Dates
